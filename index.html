How does it work?

  • Data Collection & Classification

    We've compiled detailed information on credit card offers from over 10 leading US banks. The data is organized based on key factors such as cash back options, point systems, APR rates, required credit scores, and exclusive perks.

  • Algorithm

    Our proprietary algorithm processes this data alongside your answers from our web form to pinpoint the credit card that aligns with your spending habits and credit needs.

    Receive a personalized credit card recommendation that optimizes your rewards and benefits based on your actual spending patterns.
